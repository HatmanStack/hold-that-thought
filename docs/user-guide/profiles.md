# Managing Your Profile

Your profile helps family members learn about you and see your contributions to the family history.

## Setting Up Your Profile

### First Time Setup

1. Click your name or avatar in the top right corner
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Fill in your information:
   - **Display Name:** Your preferred name (required)
   - **Bio:** Tell your story (up to 500 characters)
   - **Family Relationship:** How you're related (e.g., "Daughter", "Grandson")
   - **Profile Photo:** Upload a photo of yourself

4. Click "Save Changes"

### Uploading a Profile Photo

1. In Profile Settings, click "Upload Photo" or the camera icon
2. Select an image from your computer (JPG or PNG)
3. Maximum file size: 5MB
4. The photo will be automatically resized and cropped to a square
5. Click "Save" to confirm

- Use a clear, recent photo of your face

## Viewing Profiles

### Your Own Profile

1. Click your name in the top navigation
2. Or go to Profile Settings and click "View Public Profile"
3. You'll see:
   - Your profile photo and name
   - Your bio and family relationship
   - Activity stats (comments posted, photos uploaded)
   - Your comment history

### Other Family Members' Profiles

1. Click on any family member's name or photo (in comments, etc.)
2. You'll see their public profile information
3. If their profile is private, you'll see limited information

## Editing Your Profile

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Update any field you want to change
3. Click "Save Changes"
4. Changes take effect immediately

**Editable Fields:**
- Display name
- Bio
- Family relationship
- Profile photo
- Privacy settings

## Profile Privacy

### Making Your Profile Private

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Toggle "Make my profile private"
3. Click "Save Changes"

**What this does:**
- Only you and admins can see your full profile
- Others see only your name and profile photo
- Your comments are still visible to all family members

[Learn more about Privacy Settings](./privacy.md)

## Your Comment History

Your profile shows all comments you've posted:

1. Go to your profile page
2. Scroll to "Comment History" section
3. Click any comment to go to the original letter or photo
4. See reactions your comments received

**Stats shown:**
- Total comments posted
- Total reactions received
- Most active on (which letters/photos)

## Activity Stats

Your profile displays:
- **Comments Posted:** Total number of comments
- **Photos Uploaded:** Total media contributions
- **Member Since:** When you joined
- **Last Active:** When you last logged in

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, in Profile Settings update your email and verify the new address.

**Q: How do I delete my profile?**
A: Contact an administrator. Your profile and data will be permanently removed.

**Q: Why isn't my profile photo showing?**
A: Make sure the image is under 5MB and in JPG or PNG format. Try uploading again.

**Q: Can I have multiple profiles?**
A: No, one profile per person. Contact admin if you need to merge duplicate accounts.

**Q: Who can see my profile?**
A: All approved family members can see public profiles. Private profiles are only visible to you and admins.

## Tips for Great Profiles

1. **Complete your bio:** Share your story - where you live, what you do, favorite family memories
2. **Use a real photo:** Helps family members recognize you
3. **Keep it updated:** Update your bio as life changes
4. **Be yourself:** This is family - be authentic!

Example bio:
> "Father of two, grandfather of five. Living in Seattle since 2010. I love woodworking and collecting family recipes. My favorite family memory is the summers at the lake house."
