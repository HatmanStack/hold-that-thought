# How to Use Comments

Comments allow you to share your thoughts and memories about family letters and photos.

## Adding a Comment

1. Navigate to any letter (e.g., /2015/christmas) or photo in the gallery
2. Scroll to the comment section at the bottom of the page
3. Click in the text box where it says "Write a comment..."
4. Type your comment (up to 2000 characters)
5. Click the "Post Comment" or "Post" button
6. Your comment will appear immediately at the bottom of the list

**Tips:**
- Be respectful and kind in your comments
- Share specific memories or reactions
- Ask questions to engage other family members
- Keep comments family-friendly

## Reacting to Comments

Show appreciation for others' comments by reacting:

1. Find the comment you want to react to
2. Click the heart (‚ô•) or thumbs up (üëç) icon
3. The reaction count will increase by 1
4. Click again to remove your reaction

**Note:** You can only add one reaction per comment. Clicking again toggles it off.

## Editing Your Comments

Made a typo or want to add more? You can edit your own comments:

1. Find your comment in the list
2. Click the "Edit" button (pencil icon) next to your comment
3. Make your changes in the text box
4. Click "Save" to update or "Cancel" to discard changes
5. Edited comments show an "(edited)" label

**Note:** You cannot edit others' comments. Only admins can delete inappropriate comments.

## Deleting Your Comments

Changed your mind? You can delete your own comments:

1. Find your comment in the list
2. Click the "Delete" button (trash icon)
3. Confirm the deletion if prompted
4. The comment will be removed immediately

**Warning:** Deleted comments cannot be recovered!

## Viewing All Comments

Comments are displayed in chronological order (newest at the bottom):

- Scroll through the list to read all comments
- If there are many comments, click "Load More" to see older ones
- Each comment shows:
  - Commenter's name and profile photo
  - Comment text
  - Timestamp (e.g., "2 hours ago")
  - Number of reactions
  - Edit/Delete buttons (on your own comments only)

## Comment Notifications

Get notified when someone responds to your comments:

- Email notifications are sent when someone reacts to your comment
- Check your email inbox for new activity
- Click the link in the email to view the letter and comments

**Tip:** You can manage notification preferences in your Profile Settings.

## Comment Guidelines

**Do:**
- Share meaningful memories and stories
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Express appreciation and love
- Correct factual information politely

**Don't:**
- Post offensive or inappropriate content
- Share personal information (addresses, phone numbers)
- Spam or post repetitive comments
- Attack or criticize family members

**Violations:** Inappropriate comments will be removed by administrators.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long can my comment be?**
A: Comments can be up to 2000 characters (about 300-400 words).

**Q: Can I add photos to my comments?**
A: Not currently. You can describe photos or reference photos already in the gallery.

**Q: Why can't I see the comment button?**
A: Make sure you're logged in and your account has been approved by an administrator.

**Q: Can I comment on old letters?**
A: Yes! All letters, regardless of age, can be commented on.

**Q: Who can see my comments?**
A: Only approved family members who are logged in can see comments.

**Q: Can I mention someone in a comment?**
A: @mentions are not currently supported, but may be added in the future.

**Q: How do I know if someone responded to my comment?**
A: You'll receive an email notification if someone reacts to your comment or posts a new comment on the same item.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Comment won't post

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're still logged in (refresh the page)
- Verify your comment isn't empty or over 2000 characters
- Try refreshing the page and posting again

**Problem:** Can't edit or delete comment

**Possible causes:**
- You're trying to edit someone else's comment (only your own can be edited)
- The comment is too old (there may be a time limit)
- You're not logged in

**Problem:** Comments not loading

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging out and back in
- Clear your browser cache

## Tips for Great Comments

1. **Be specific:** "I remember that Christmas - we had just moved to the new house!" is better than "Nice letter."

2. **Ask questions:** "Dad, what was grandpa's reaction when he opened the gift?"

3. **Share related memories:** "This reminds me of the time we..."

4. **Express gratitude:** "Thank you for sharing this letter. It means so much to our family history."

5. **Add context:** "For those who don't know, this was right after Uncle John returned from overseas."
