## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the Hold That Thought website
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Wait for admin approval (family members only)

### First Time Login

After your account is approved:
1. Click "Log In"
2. Enter your email and password
3. Complete your profile with:
   - Your name
   - Profile photo
   - Family relationship (e.g., "Daughter", "Grandson")
   - Short bio

## FAQ

### How do I get approved?

After signing up, a family administrator will review your request. This usually takes 1-2 business days. You'll receive an email when approved.

### Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Profile Settings and click "Delete Account" at the bottom. This will remove all your data permanently.

### Are my messages private?

Yes. Only you and the recipients can see your direct messages. Family administrators cannot read your private messages.

### Can I change my email address?

Yes. Go to Profile Settings and update your email. You'll need to verify the new address.

### How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the three-dot menu on any comment or message and select "Report". Family administrators will review your report.

### Can I download family letters?

Currently, letters must be viewed online. A download feature may be added in the future.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

- Verify your email address (check spam folder for verification email)
- Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link
- Ensure your account has been approved by an administrator
- Try clearing your browser cache and cookies

### Comments won't post

- Check that your comment is under 2000 characters
- Ensure you're logged in
- Refresh the page and try again
- If the issue persists, contact support

### Images won't upload

- Ensure your image is under 10MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
- Check your internet connection
- Try using a different browser

### Messages not sending

- Verify you've selected a recipient
- Check that your message isn't empty
- Ensure you're logged in
- Refresh the page and try again
