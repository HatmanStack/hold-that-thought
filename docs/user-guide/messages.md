# Using Direct Messages

Send private messages to family members to stay connected.

## Viewing Your Inbox

1. Click "Messages" in the top navigation
2. You'll see your list of conversations
3. Unread messages show a blue dot or badge
4. Conversations are sorted by most recent activity

## Starting a New Conversation

### One-on-One Message

1. Click "New Message" or the compose icon
2. Select a family member from the list
3. Type your message (up to 5000 characters)
4. Click "Send"

### Group Conversation

1. Click "New Message"
2. Select "Group" option
3. Choose multiple family members
4. Give the group a name (optional, e.g., "Smith Family Planning")
5. Type your first message
6. Click "Send"

## Sending Messages

1. Open a conversation
2. Type your message in the text box at the bottom
3. Press Enter or click "Send"

**Tips:**
- Keep messages respectful and family-friendly
- Use line breaks for longer messages

## Adding Attachments

Share photos and documents in your messages:

1. Click the paperclip or attachment icon
2. Select file(s) from your computer
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF, DOC, TXT
4. Maximum size: 10MB per file, up to 5 files per message
5. Click "Send" to send message with attachments

**Note:** Attachments are stored securely and only visible to conversation participants.

## Viewing Conversation History

1. Open any conversation
2. Scroll up to see older messages
3. Click "Load More" if there are many messages
4. Messages show:
   - Sender's name and photo
   - Message text
   - Attachments (if any)
   - Timestamp

## Deleting Conversations

1. In your inbox, find the conversation
2. Swipe left (mobile) or click the menu icon
3. Select "Delete"
4. Confirm deletion

**Warning:** This removes the conversation from your inbox but doesn't delete messages for other participants.

## Message Notifications

**Email Notifications:**
- You'll receive an email when someone sends you a message
- Emails include the sender's name and a preview
- Click the link to view the full message

**Managing Notifications:**
1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Find "Notification Preferences"
3. Toggle message notifications on/off

## Message Privacy

- Only conversation participants can see messages
- **Administrators cannot** read your private messages
- Messages are encrypted in transit and at rest
- Deleted conversations are permanently removed from your account

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I edit or delete sent messages?**
A: Not currently. Double-check before sending!

**Q: How long are messages kept?**
A: Messages are kept indefinitely unless you delete the conversation.

**Q: Can I send messages to multiple people at once?**
A: Yes, create a group conversation.

**Q: What's the difference between messages and comments?**
A: Messages are private (only participants see them). Comments are public (all family members see them).

**Q: Why can't I message someone?**
A: They may have left the group, or their account may be inactive. Contact an administrator.

**Q: Can I send videos?**
A: Currently only photos, PDFs, and documents are supported. Videos may be added in the future.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Messages not sending

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're still logged in
- Verify you selected a recipient
- Try refreshing the page

**Problem:** Can't see attachments

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you're online
- Try downloading the attachment
- Check file format is supported
- Contact sender if issue persists

**Problem:** Not receiving email notifications

**Solutions:**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify notification settings in Profile
- Ensure your email address is correct
- Add noreply@holdthatthought.family to contacts

## Tips for Effective Messaging

1. **Be clear:** State your purpose upfront
2. **Use groups:** For family planning, coordinate events efficiently
3. **Share photos:** Attachments make messages more engaging
4. **Respond promptly:** Keep conversations flowing
5. **Be respectful:** Remember this is family communication
