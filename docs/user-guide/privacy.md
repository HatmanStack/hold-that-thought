# Privacy Settings & Security

Your privacy and security are important to us. Learn how to control who sees your information.

## What Information Is Collected

**Profile Information:**
- Name and email address (required)
- Profile photo (optional)
- Bio and family relationship (optional)

**Activity Data:**
- Comments you post
- Reactions you give
- Messages you send
- Photos you upload
- Login history

**Automatically Collected:**
- Browser type and version
- IP address (for security)
- Device information
- Access times and dates

## Who Can See Your Information

### Public to All Family Members

- Your name and profile photo
- Your public comments on letters and photos
- Your reactions on comments
- Your activity stats (number of comments, etc.)

### Private (Only You Can See)

- Your private messages
- Your email address
- Your login history
- Your draft messages

### Visible to Administrators Only

- Full account details
- Moderation history
- Security logs
- Technical metadata

**Important:** Administrators cannot read your private messages.

## Privacy Controls

### Making Your Profile Private

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Enable "Make my profile private"
3. Click "Save Changes"

**What changes:**
- ✓ Others see only your name and photo
- ✓ Your bio and stats are hidden
- ✓ Your comment history is hidden
- ✓ Your comments on letters remain visible (with name and photo)

**What doesn't change:**
- Comments you've already posted
- Messages you've sent
- Your ability to interact with others

### Controlling Notifications

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Find "Notification Preferences"
3. Toggle each notification type:
   - Email when someone comments on same letter
   - Email when someone reacts to your comment
   - Email when you receive a direct message
   - Weekly summary of activity

4. Click "Save Changes"

## Account Security

### Password Best Practices

- Use at least 8 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
- Don't reuse passwords from other sites
- Change your password every 6-12 months

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click "Update Password"

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For extra security, enable 2FA:

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Find "Security" section
3. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
4. Scan QR code with authenticator app
5. Enter confirmation code

**With 2FA enabled:** You'll need both your password and a code from your phone to log in.

### Active Sessions

View and manage where you're logged in:

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Click "Active Sessions"
3. See list of devices
4. Click "Sign Out" to end any session

## Data Rights

### Accessing Your Data

You have the right to:
- View all data we have about you
- Export your data in a portable format
- Correct inaccurate information

**To request your data:**
Email: privacy@holdthatthought.family

### Deleting Your Data

You have the right to delete your account:

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion (this is permanent!)

**What gets deleted:**
- Your profile and personal information
- Your comments (replaced with "[deleted]")
- Your private messages
- Your activity history

**What remains:**
- Comments you posted (as "[deleted user]")
- Photos you uploaded (if publicly shared)

## Family Access Policy

**Who can join:**
- Only approved family members
- Must be verified by an administrator
- No public registration

**Who can see content:**
- Only logged-in, approved family members
- Content is not public or searchable online
- No external access without account

## Data Security Measures

**We protect your data with:**
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest (AWS KMS)
- Secure cloud hosting (AWS)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and logging
- Automatic backups

**We never:**
- Sell your data
- Share data with third parties (except AWS for hosting)
- Use data for advertising
- Track you across other websites

## Cookies & Tracking

**Essential Cookies:** (Required for site to function)
- Authentication tokens
- Session management
- Security features

**Optional Cookies:** (Can be disabled)
- Analytics (anonymized usage data)
- Preference settings

**No tracking cookies:** We don't use advertising or tracking cookies.

**To manage cookies:**
- Use your browser's cookie settings
- Clear cookies to sign out all devices

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can administrators read my messages?**
A: No. Private messages are encrypted and only visible to participants.

**Q: Can I delete old comments?**
A: Yes, click "Delete" on any of your comments.

**Q: Who sees my email address?**
A: Only you and administrators. It's not shown to other family members.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: Yes, we maintain secure backups for 30 days.

**Q: Can I use a pseudonym?**
A: We prefer real names, but nicknames are acceptable with admin approval.

**Q: What happens if I don't log in for a while?**
A: Your account remains active indefinitely. No automatic deletion.
